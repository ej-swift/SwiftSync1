SwiftSync

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Overview

SwiftSync is a desktop application that runs on your computer. We designed it so your streaming credentials stay on your PC when you use the desktop app locally. If you use chat-only on your phone, sign-in tokens for that session may be stored on the optional cloud relay server (see below).

Information we collect

SwiftSync does not operate a central user database. The App may store locally on your device:

  • OBS WebSocket connection settings
  • Chat platform OAuth tokens and API keys you provide
  • Pairing codes for connecting your phone to your PC
  • Chat messages displayed while you use the App

TikTok Login Kit

If you choose to sign in with TikTok, SwiftSync requests the user.info.basic scope to read your TikTok username and display name so we can auto-fill your account in the App. Authorization tokens are stored locally in your SwiftSync user data folder on your PC. We do not sell TikTok profile data.

Third-party services

When you connect Twitch, Kick, YouTube, TikTok, EulerStream, or other services, those providers process data according to their own privacy policies. SwiftSync sends requests only as needed to operate features you enable.

Cloud relay (optional)

If you enable the optional cloud relay, pairing and command traffic may pass through a relay server you configure. Chat content may be cached briefly for mobile access.

If you use chat-only mode on your phone (no PC), each phone gets its own private pairing room on the cloud relay. OAuth tokens you approve for Twitch, Kick, YouTube, or TikTok are stored on that relay server only for your room, so chat works while the mobile page is open. SwiftSync does not operate a central user account database.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data leaves your computer only when you connect to third-party APIs, optional relay infrastructure, or when required by law.

Your choices

You can disconnect platforms at any time, delete stored config files from your SwiftSync user data folder, or uninstall the App to remove local data.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the maintainer through the GitHub repository associated with this project.
