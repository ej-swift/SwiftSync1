How SwiftSync works

Three steps from install to controlling your stream from your phone.

1

Install on your streaming PC

Download SwiftSync for Windows, connect it to OBS via WebSocket, and sign in to the chat platforms you use.

2

Pair your phone

Open the mobile control page on your phone and scan the QR code or enter the pairing code shown in SwiftSync.

3

Control while live

Change scenes, adjust audio, read chat, and send messages from your phone while SwiftSync runs on your PC.

TikTok Login Kit integration

SwiftSync uses TikTok Login Kit on the desktop app so streamers can connect their TikTok account securely.

What we request: TikTok scope user.info.basic only.

  • Used to read your TikTok username and display name after you sign in on your PC.
  • Auto-fills your TikTok account in SwiftSync chat settings.
  • Tokens are stored locally on your computer — SwiftSync does not operate a central user database.
  • Live chat while you are broadcasting uses your TikTok username; you must be LIVE to send messages.

Frequently asked questions

Is SwiftSync a web app or a desktop app?

SwiftSync is primarily a Windows desktop application. This website describes the product and hosts our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. The mobile experience runs in your phone browser after pairing with your PC.

Does SwiftSync store my TikTok password?

No. TikTok sign-in uses Login Kit OAuth in the desktop app. SwiftSync receives an access token for the scopes you approve and stores it locally on your PC.

Can I use SwiftSync away from my home network?

Yes. SwiftSync supports an optional cloud relay so your phone can connect over cellular data while your PC stays online at home or in your studio.

How do I get support?

Open an issue on GitHub Issues or contact the developer through the TikTok Developer Portal support channel used for app review.
