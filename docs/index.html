SwiftSync

Control your stream from anywhere

SwiftSync is a Windows desktop app that pairs with your phone so you can switch OBS scenes, adjust audio, and read or send multi-platform chat while you stream.

Features

  • OBS scene and source control
  • Global audio mixer controls
  • Multi-platform chat (Twitch, Kick, YouTube, TikTok)
  • Secure pairing between your PC and mobile browser

TikTok Login Kit

SwiftSync uses TikTok Login Kit so streamers can sign in on their own PC and auto-fill their TikTok username. We request user.info.basic only. Tokens are stored locally on the user's computer.
